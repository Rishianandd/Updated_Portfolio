Paper Publication

This paper presents a thorough exploration into the design, development, and evaluation of a state-of-the-art Speech-Recognized Voice Bot. The research aims to contribute to the burgeoning field of natural language processing and human-computer interaction by introducing an advanced system that leverages cutting-edge speech recognition technology. The study encompasses the entire lifecycle of the Voice Bot, from its conceptualization and architecture to the rigorous evaluation of its performance.
